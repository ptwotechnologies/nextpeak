Home Services Contract Drafting & Negotiation

Secure Your Deals. Safeguard Your Interests.

Contracts are the foundation of every business relationship whether it's with co-founders, clients, vendors, investors, or employees. At NexPeak Legal Partners, we help startups draft, vet, and negotiate contracts that are legally sound, business-friendly, and future-ready.

Why It Matters

A poorly drafted contract can lead to disputes, delays, or even financial losses. Our legal team ensures that every agreement is:

  • Legally enforceable
  • Clearly worded
  • Commercially fair
  • Compliant with applicable laws

We don't just draft we think ahead, minimizing risk and maximizing protection for your business.

Our Contract Services Include:

  • Founders' & Co-Founders' Agreements: Clarify equity splits, roles, exit rights, and dispute resolution among startup founders.
  • Employment & Consultancy Contracts: Legally compliant agreements for employees, freelancers, and advisors, covering IP, confidentiality, and termination.
  • Vendor & Client Agreements: Clearly define scope of work, deliverables, payments, and liabilities to avoid future misunderstandings.
  • NDAs (Non-Disclosure Agreements): Protect sensitive business information during pitches, hiring, or collaborations.
  • Term Sheets & Shareholder Agreements: Essential for investor rounds ensuring founder protection, voting rights, and exit terms are aligned.
  • Service Level Agreements (SLAs): Set expectations with service providers and clients to avoid performance and payment issues.
  • Licensing & IP Assignment Agreements: Clearly outline how intellectual property is used, shared, or transferred especially important for tech and content startups.

Other Contract Negotiation Support

  • Vendor or client deal finalizations
