Digital Business & E-Commerce Law

Home Services Digital Business & E-Commerce Law

Legal Backbone for Your Online Ventures

As the digital marketplace grows, so do the legal complexities that come with it. At NexPeak, we provide end-to-end legal support to online businesses, e-commerce platforms, and digital entrepreneurs to ensure smooth, compliant, and risk-free operations.

Our Services Include:

1. Website & App Legal Documentation

Drafting Terms of Use, Privacy Policies, Return & Refund Policies, and Disclaimers to comply with IT and consumer laws.

  • Terms of Use and Privacy Policies
  • Return & Refund Policies and Disclaimers

2. Platform & Vendor Agreements

Structuring B2B, B2C, marketplace, and aggregator models with platform usage terms, vendor onboarding contracts, and service agreements.

  • B2B, B2C, marketplace, and aggregator terms
  • Vendor onboarding and service agreements

3. Consumer Protection Compliance

Ensuring adherence to the Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules, 2020, including grievance redressal and transparency standards.

  • Compliance with E-Commerce Rules, 2020
  • Grievance redressal and transparency standards

4. Payment Gateway & Logistics Agreements

Advising on agreements with payment service providers, digital wallets, and third-party logistics partners.

  • Agreements with payment service providers and digital wallets
  • Third-party logistics partner agreements

5. Advertising & Influencer Guidelines

Legal review of marketing campaigns, disclaimers, and influencer collaborations in line with ASCI and digital advertising norms.

  • Legal review of marketing campaigns and disclaimers
  • Influencer collaborations and digital advertising norms

6. Data Privacy & IT Compliance

Ensuring secure user data handling, cookie consent mechanisms, and compliance with applicable tech and privacy laws.

  • Secure user data handling and cookie consent
  • Compliance with tech and privacy laws

7. IP & Brand Registration for Marketplace Sellers

Filing and securing trademark registration to activate or verify seller accounts on e-commerce platforms (like Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho). Includes brand protection strategies and assistance with brand registry portals.

  • Trademark registration for seller accounts
  • Brand protection strategies and registry assistance
