In today’s knowledge-driven economy, your brand, content, innovation, or technology is more than just an asset—it’s your business identity and market differentiator. Our Key IPR Services

1. Trademark Registration

Comprehensive support for wordmarks, logos, slogans, and brand names—from availability search and filing to opposition and renewal.

  • Availability search, filing, and renewal
  • Protection for wordmarks, logos, slogans

2. Copyright Registration

Protection for your original works such as website content, software code, designs, marketing materials, and creative assets.

  • Website content, software code, designs
  • Marketing materials and creative assets

3. Patent Filing Support

Advisory and assistance in drafting and filing provisional and complete patent applications for inventions and technical innovations.

  • Drafting provisional applications
  • Filing complete patent applications

4. Design Registration

Legal protection for product shapes, graphical elements, packaging designs, and unique visual expressions.

  • Product shapes and graphical elements
  • Packaging designs and visual expressions

5. Trade Secret Protection

Secure internal processes, formulas, or data through NDAs, confidentiality clauses, and trade secret documentation frameworks.

  • NDAs and confidentiality clauses
  • Trade secret documentation frameworks
