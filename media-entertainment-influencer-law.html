Legal Expertise for the Creative Industry

The media, entertainment, and influencer sectors are dynamic and constantly evolving, presenting unique legal challenges. NexPeak offers specialized legal services to production houses, artists, content creators, influencers, and digital platforms, ensuring their creative and commercial endeavors are legally sound and protected.

Our Services Include:

  • Content Licensing & Distribution: Drafting and reviewing agreements for content licensing, syndication, and distribution across various platforms.
  • Artist & Talent Agreements: Structuring contracts for artists, musicians, actors, and other talent, including management, performance, and recording agreements.
  • Influencer Marketing Compliance: Advising influencers and brands on disclosure requirements, advertising standards, and regulatory compliance (e.g., ASCI guidelines).
  • Intellectual Property Rights: Protecting copyrights, trademarks, and personality rights for creative works, brand names, and public figures.
  • Defamation & Reputation Management: Providing legal counsel on matters of libel, slander, and online reputation management.
  • Film & Television Production Legal: Assisting with pre-production, production, and post-production legalities, including clearances, financing, and co-production agreements.
  • Digital Platform Legal Advisory: Counseling on terms of service, user-generated content policies, and compliance for streaming services, social media, and gaming platforms.
