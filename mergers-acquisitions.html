Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A) and Corporate Restructuring

Home Services Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A) and Corporate Restructuring

Strategic Legal Support for Business Expansion, Consolidation & Transformation

Whether you're scaling through acquisitions, consolidating operations, or restructuring your corporate framework, the legal complexity of such transactions demands precision, foresight, and strategic execution. NexPeak offers end-to-end legal advisory for M&A deals and corporate restructuring to help you navigate these high-stakes transitions with clarity and compliance.

Our Services Include:

1. M&A Transaction Advisory

Comprehensive legal support for mergers, acquisitions, joint ventures, asset purchases, and share purchases—covering all stages from due diligence to post-deal integration.

  • Comprehensive legal support for various M&A types
  • Guidance from due diligence to post-deal integration

2. Legal Due Diligence

In-depth review of corporate, financial, regulatory, tax, and litigation aspects to assess risk and value in potential targets or buyers.

  • Risk and value assessment
  • Review of corporate, financial, regulatory, tax, and litigation aspects

3. Deal Structuring & Strategy

Advising on optimal legal and tax-efficient structures for domestic and cross-border transactions, ensuring regulatory and commercial alignment.

  • Optimal legal and tax-efficient structures
  • Ensuring regulatory and commercial alignment

4. Drafting & Negotiation of Transaction Documents

Preparing and negotiating critical documents including Term Sheets, Share Purchase Agreements (SPA), Shareholders’ Agreements (SHA), Business Transfer Agreements (BTA), and Non-Compete Clauses.

  • Drafting and negotiation of critical transaction documents
  • Expertise in SPAs, SHAs, BTAs, and Non-Compete Clauses

5. Regulatory Filings & Approvals

Handling approvals and filings with regulators such as the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA), Reserve Bank of India (RBI), Competition Commission of India (CCI), and SEBI where applicable.

  • Securing approvals from key regulatory bodies
  • Compliance with MCA, RBI, CCI, and SEBI regulations

6. Corporate Restructuring & Reorganization

Legal support for internal group reorganizations, demergers, amalgamations, slump sales, and hive-offs to optimize operational efficiency and compliance.

  • Support for internal group reorganizations
  • Demergers, amalgamations, slump sales, and hive-offs

7. Post-Merger Integration & Compliance

Support in aligning HR, IP, tax, and governance frameworks post-transaction to ensure seamless continuity of operations and compliance.

  • Aligning HR, IP, tax, and governance frameworks
  • Ensuring seamless continuity and compliance post-transaction

8. Cross-Border Transactions

Advising on foreign investment, inbound and outbound structuring, FEMA compliance, and related international legal frameworks.

  • Foreign investment and structuring advice
  • FEMA compliance and international legal frameworks

9. Employee & ESOP Structuring During M&A

Protecting employee rights and structuring ESOP transitions during mergers, acquisitions, and reorganizations.

  • Protection of employee rights during M&A
  • Structuring ESOP transitions

10. Risk Mitigation & Dispute Handling

Advising on potential disputes, indemnities, and representations & warranties to safeguard the client’s position throughout the transaction lifecycle.

  • Advising on potential disputes and indemnities
  • Safeguarding client's position through representations & warranties
