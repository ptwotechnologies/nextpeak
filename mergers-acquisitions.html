Home Services Media, Entertainment & Influencer Law

Legal Expertise for the Creative Industry

The media, entertainment, and influencer sectors are dynamic and constantly evolving, presenting unique legal challenges. NexPeak offers specialized legal services to production houses, artists, content creators, influencers, and digital platforms, ensuring their creative and commercial endeavors are legally sound and protected.

Our Services Include:

1. Content Licensing & Distribution

Drafting and reviewing agreements for content licensing, syndication, and distribution across various platforms.

  • Content licensing agreements
  • Syndication and distribution contracts

2. Artist & Talent Agreements

Structuring contracts for artists, musicians, actors, and other talent, including management, performance, and recording agreements.

  • Artist management contracts
  • Performance and recording agreements

3. Influencer Marketing Compliance

Advising influencers and brands on disclosure requirements, advertising standards, and regulatory compliance (e.g., ASCI guidelines).

  • Disclosure requirements for influencers
  • ASCI guidelines and advertising standards

4. Intellectual Property Rights

Protecting copyrights, trademarks, and personality rights for creative works, brand names, and public figures.

  • Copyright and trademark protection
  • Personality rights for public figures

5. Defamation & Reputation Management

Providing legal counsel on matters of libel, slander, and online reputation management.

  • Libel and slander advisory
  • Online reputation management

6. Film & Television Production Legal

Assisting with pre-production, production, and post-production legalities, including clearances, financing, and co-production agreements.

  • Production legalities and clearances
  • Financing and co-production agreements

7. Digital Platform Legal Advisory

Counseling on terms of service, user-generated content policies, and compliance for streaming services, social media, and gaming platforms.

  • Terms of service and UGC policies
  • Compliance for streaming and gaming platforms
