Startup Advisory & Incorporation

Home Services Startup Advisory & Incorporation

Empowering Startups with End-to-End Legal and Compliance Solutions

Launching a startup is a bold and exciting journey—but it also comes with a maze of legal, regulatory, and operational challenges. At NexPeak Legal Partners, we specialize in guiding startups from ideation to expansion with tailored legal solutions that are not only cost-effective but strategically aligned with business growth. Whether you’re at the seed stage, ready to scale, or preparing for funding, we serve as your trusted legal backbone every step of the way.

A detailed overview of the services we provide to startups:

1. Business Incorporation and Structuring

The first step in your startup journey is choosing the right business structure. Our expert team assists in:

  • Entity Selection: Guidance on whether to opt for a Private Limited Company, LLP, OPC, or Partnership based on your goals.
  • Incorporation Process: Complete documentation, name approval, and filing with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA).
  • Founders’ Agreement: Drafting MOA, AOA, and founders' agreements to clearly define roles, equity, and responsibilities.
  • PAN, TAN & GST Registration: We handle all the mandatory initial registrations to ensure you're fully compliant from day one.

2. Compliance and Regulatory Support

Compliance is non-negotiable, and failing to meet your obligations can result in penalties or business interruption. We offer ongoing support for:

  • ROC Filings: Annual filings such as AOC-4, MGT-7, and DIR-3 KYC to meet Companies Act requirements.
  • GST Compliance: Monthly/quarterly GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, and annual returns along with timely GST computation.
  • TDS & EPF/ESIC Returns: Complete management of payroll-related filings and deductions.
  • Statutory Registers & Minutes Maintenance: We maintain all your registers, resolutions, and board meeting documents.

We provide subscription-based plans that cover all routine legal filings and offer you the flexibility to choose based on your scale and budget.

3. Drafting of Legal Documents & Agreements

Legal documentation forms the foundation of every professional business relationship. We help draft and vet:

  • Founder and Co-founder Agreements
  • Employment Offer Letters and Contracts
  • Vendor and Client Agreements
  • Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs)
  • Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policies for websites and apps
  • Shareholders’ and Share Subscription Agreements
  • Service Level Agreements (SLAs)

Our legal team ensures your interests are well-protected while maintaining compliance with Indian laws.

4. Intellectual Property (IPR) Protection

Your brand, content, software, or invention could be your most valuable asset. We provide comprehensive IP protection services:

  • Trademark Registration: Search, filing, and opposition handling for your brand name or logo.
  • Copyright Filing: For your creative assets—content, software code, website, and more.
  • Patent Support: Guidance in drafting and filing provisional and complete patents for your inventions.
  • IP Licensing & Assignment Agreements: Helping you monetize and protect your IP portfolio.

With us, you don’t just file for protection—you gain strategic guidance to enforce and commercially use your IP.

5. Startup India & DPIIT Registration

We help eligible startups obtain recognition under the Startup India scheme:

  • DPIIT Registration: Fast-track recognition to avail tax benefits, easier compliance, and IPR rebates.
  • Tax Exemption Filings: Assistance with forms 80 IAC and 56(2)(viib) for angel tax exemption and profit deductions.
  • Pitch Deck and Business Model Review: To ensure your registration application stands strong.

Our experience ensures a smooth process and increased chances of approval.

6. Funding and Investment Support

Raising capital is a significant milestone in a startup’s journey. We provide legal assistance across the fundraising cycle:

  • Due Diligence Support: Preparing your startup for investor scrutiny with proper documentation and disclosures.
  • Drafting of Term Sheets & SHA/SSA: Ensuring terms are investor-friendly but founder-safe.
  • Cap Table Structuring: Advising on equity distribution, dilution impact, and ESOP planning.
  • SAFE/Convertible Note Drafting: For early-stage investments and bridge rounds.

We aim to protect your long-term equity while facilitating seamless investor relations.

7. Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOPs)

Attracting and retaining talent can be challenging. ESOPs are a smart way to offer ownership without immediate cash payouts. We offer:

  • ESOP Scheme Design & Policy Drafting
  • Board & Shareholder Resolutions
  • Trust Formation (if required)
  • Valuation Support and Tax Advisory
  • Employee Communication and Grant Letters

We ensure the ESOPs are legally compliant, tax-efficient, and tailored to your compensation strategy.

8. Website & App Compliance

In today’s digital world, startups must adhere to online regulations to avoid data privacy or consumer protection issues. We help with:

  • Privacy Policy & Terms of Use Drafting
  • Cookie Policy and Consent Mechanisms
  • E-commerce and Payment Gateway Agreements
  • IT Act and Intermediary Guidelines Compliance

Especially for tech, SaaS, and e-commerce startups, our services ensure smooth and risk-free digital operations.

9. Sector-Specific Licensing & Advisory

Many industries require special licenses or legal compliance. We guide startups across sectors like:

  • Fintech: RBI regulations, payment aggregator registration.
  • Edtech: Content licensing, online education compliance.
  • Healthtech: Telemedicine guidelines, data privacy, medical device licensing.
  • E-commerce: Consumer protection laws, marketplace agreements.

We stay ahead of industry developments so you can focus on innovation.

10. Ongoing Legal Advisory & Support

Startups evolve fast and need quick, reliable legal help. Our retainer-based subscription models ensure:

  • 24x7 Legal Consultation Support
  • Contract Review and Negotiation Help
  • Crisis and Risk Management
  • Pre-litigation Dispute Resolution

Whether you need urgent advice on a client conflict or clarity on a government notice, we're just a call away.
